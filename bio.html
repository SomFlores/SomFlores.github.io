▾ ▾ bio ▾ ▾

Tiago Aires (Rita*) Lêdo , born in 1992, is a media artist from Porto and part of the media and sound arts collective - COLECTIVO 「大音量コンピューター」(loud volume computer).

Interested in militant sound works, generative music and interaction, he has participated in film, television, videogame, music, installation and educational projects.

Tiago holds a MSc in Multimedia — Interactive Music and Sound Design, from the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto (FEUP); and a Bachelor's degree in Cinema and Audiovisual from the Higher School of Arts of Porto (ESAP).

His music works have been released under the aliases rizumirai, 空のリズム, ghostrhythm and Som Flores.

He completed the Introduction to Acting Course of Teatro Universitário do Porto (TUP), and he currently teaches musical technology/programming at L.E.M (Music Experimentation Laboratory) for the Orquestra Jazz de Matosinhos' educational program (CARA OJM).
